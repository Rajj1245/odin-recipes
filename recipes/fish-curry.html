Description

A tangy and spicy coconut-based curry, flavored with Goan spices and tamarind, traditionally served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 500 g firm white fish (kingfish, pomfret, or mackerel), cleaned
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup grated coconut (or coconut milk)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 inch ginger
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • Salt, to taste

STEPS

  1. Make Masala Paste:Grind coconut, red chilies, coriander seeds, cumin, garlic, ginger, and turmeric with a little water into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook Curry:Heat oil, saut√© onion until soft. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy. Stir in masala paste, fry 2-3 mins.
  3. Add Fish:Pour 1+1/2 cups water, bring to boil, then add fish and salt. Simmer 6-8 mins until fish is cooked.
  4. Finish:Stir in tamarind pulp, simmer 1 min. Serve hot with steamed rice.
